About Us

Behind the Vision: The WHIZZ Dream Team

WHIZZ Design Services' story unfurls as an intense dedication to artistic brilliance, with a singular team focus. Spawned from the common desire to establish a sanctuary for design, WHIZZ excels in transforming your ideas into compelling visual stories. Founded in January 2023, with a pledge to superior quality, WHIZZ Design Services expertly specializes in custom-tailored sports clothing and unique designs.

Despite being a recent addition to the industry, WHIZZ Design Services presents a team with a combined expertise spanning over 5 years. Our experienced professionals have not only sharpened their skills by working with various clients but also by maneuvering a significant amount of projects. WHIZZ arrives as an invigorating force, fusing youthful energy and abundant experience gathered through partnerships with a spectrum of clients. Our beginning may be recent, but our competence is deeply entrenched in the complexity of design, ensuring that every project reaps the benefits of a robust background of knowledge and creative skill.

At the heart of our services lies a commitment to excellence, offering a comprehensive suite of solutions tailored to meet the dynamic needs of sports and custom apparel companies. From vector conversion to embroidery digitizing, namedrop to color separation, and sublimation designs for t-shirts to website image enhancements, WHIZZ is your dedicated partner in the design and art departments.

Our vision extends beyond conventional design services, it's about cultivating a collaborative environment where creativity thrives. WHIZZ is more than a service provider, we're a creative force, channeling youthful energy and seasoned expertise into crafting designs that transcend expectations.
